The Success Heist : AI in Biotechnology

Program Name : The Success Heist

Theme : Money Heist

Organized By : Open Minds

Held At : Rajalakshmi Engineering College

Character : The Professor

Open Minds hosted "The Success Heist" at Rajalakshmi Engineering College, themed around the popular series "Money Heist." The program was designed for a diverse audience from Biotechnology and Food Technology backgrounds. The Professor, a central character in the event, delivered an eye-opening presentation on how AI is revolutionizing the biotech industry. The session covered the critical role of AI in various applications, from drug discovery to gene editing.
